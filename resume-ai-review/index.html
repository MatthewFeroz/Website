AI role positioning review

Make the resume read like an AI-native builder, not a software engineer who also does AI.

The experience is strong. The opportunity is to position it around AI adoption, agentic workflows, developer education, and practical LLM systems. That is the lane that matches modern AI Engineer, Developer Relations, Education Engineer, Developer Experience, and Forward Deployed AI roles.

AI Engineer Developer Relations Education Engineer Developer Experience Agentic Workflows LLM Apps

Highest-leverage positioning shift

Right now the resume says: software engineer who also creates AI content. The stronger version says: AI-native software engineer who builds agentic systems, teaches teams how to use them, and translates technical AI tools into real adoption.

Fix the first L3Harris bullet

Before

Was the AI Point of Contact for a sixteen-person developer team, hosting multiple workshops on how to agentically program using AI, manage a skills repository, and utilize AI to accelerate development.

After

Led AI adoption for a 16-person engineering team, running workshops on agentic coding, reusable skills, and AI-assisted development workflows using tools like Claude, Codex, and Cursor.

Fix the first How AI Works line

Before

Founded an education company focused on providing education AI content aimed at non-technical professionals.

After

Founded How AI Works, an AI education company helping non-technical professionals adopt practical AI workflows through workshops, demos, and technical content.

Rename the projects section

Use “Selected AI Work” instead of “Selected Projects.” It makes the section feel intentional and aligned with AI roles.

Reorder skills

Separate AI/LLM tools from general frameworks. Recruiters should see RAG, MCP, agentic workflows, and OpenAI/Anthropic SDKs immediately.

Tighten education

Keep the MS GPA and the most relevant AI/software coursework. Remove repeated awards if space gets tight.

Recommended skills section

Languages: Python, TypeScript, JavaScript, C#, C++, SQL

AI / LLM: LangChain, MCP, Claude/OpenAI Agent SDKs, RAG, agentic workflows, prompt engineering, vector search

Backend / Infra: FastAPI, Docker, Redis, Jenkins, AWS, REST APIs

Frontend: React, HTML/CSS

Tools: Claude Code, Codex, Cursor, GitHub, Postman
